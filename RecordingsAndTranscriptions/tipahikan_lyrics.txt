peyak tipahikan nimetawân,
nimetawân, nimetawân.
peyak tipahikan nimetawân,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

nîso tipahikan nikîwân,
nikîwân, nikîwân.
nîso tipahikan nikîwân,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

nisto tipahikan nitatoskân,
nitatoskân, nitatoskân.
nisto tipahikan nitatoskân,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

newo tipahikan ninipân,
ninipân, ninipân.
newo tipahikan ninipân,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

niyânan tipahikan niwaniskân,
niwaniskân, niwaniskân.
niyânan tipahikan niwaniskân,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

nikotwâsik tipahikan nimîcison,
nimîcison, nimîcison.
nikotwâsik tipahikan nimîcison,
pônâpihtâkîsikâw.

---english translation---

One o'clock I play,
I play, I play.
one o'clock I play,
In the afternoon.

Two o'clock I go home,
I go home, I go home,
Two o'clock I go home,
In the afternoon.

Three o'clock I work,
I work, I work.
Three o'clock I work,
In the afternoon.

Four o'clock I sleep,
I sleep, I sleep.
Four o'clock I sleep,

Five o'clock I wake up,
I wake up, I wake up.
Five o'clock I wake up,
In the afternoon.

Six o'clock I eat,
I eat, I eat.
Six o'clock I eat,
In the afternoon.
