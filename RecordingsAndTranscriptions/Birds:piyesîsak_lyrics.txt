niyânan piyesîsak apiwak iskwâhtemihk,
peyak sipwepihâw,
newo ekwa piko.

newo piyesîsak miscikosa tâhkwahtamwak
peyak sipwepihâw,
nisto ekwa piko.

nisto piyesîsak kâsôwak maskisinihk,
peyak sipwepihâw,
nîso ekwa piko.

nîso piyesîsak mistahi nikamowak,
peyak sipwepihâw,
peyak ekwa piko.

peyak piyesîsis mistahi mâh-mâtow,
ewako esipwepihât,
namoya kîkway piyesîsak.

---english translation---

Five birds sitting on the door,
one flies away,
Only four left.

Four birds chewing on little sticks,
one flies away,
only three left.

Three birds hiding in a shoe,
one flies away,
only two left.

Two birds singing a lot,
one flies away,
only one left.

One little bird is crying a lot,
that one flies away,
no more birds left.
